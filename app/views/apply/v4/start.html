Apply for New Style Employment Support Allowance (ESA)

Before you apply make sure you have;

  • National Insurance number
  • Bank or building society details
  • Details of your GP/Doctor
  • Details of any Personal Pension you are receiving
  • Details of any Health Insurance plan you have
Start now

After you make your claim

You’ll get a text, phone call or email confirming your appointment for your Employment Support Allowance interview.

You’ll need all of the following for your interview:

  • one ‘primary’ identity document
  • 2 ‘secondary’ identity documents - one to prove your address and the other to prove your date of birth
You won’t get Employment Support Allowance if you don't have an interview.
