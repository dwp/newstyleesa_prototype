Apply for New Style Employment and Support Allowance (ESA)

Before you apply make sure you have:

  • your National Insurance number
  • bank or building society details
  • details of your GP surgery or medical centre
  • details of any workplace pension you are receiving
  • details of any permanent health insurance or employment income protection plan you have
Start now

After you've applied

After you submit your application you’ll need to make an appointment to:

  • prove your identity
  • agree and sign a 'claimant commitment' which is a record of the responsibilities you accept in return for receiving ESA

You'll be told how to make the appointment at the end of this application.

You won’t get Employment Support Allowance if you don't make an appointment.
