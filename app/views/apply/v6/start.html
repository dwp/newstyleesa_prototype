Apply for 'new style' Employment and Support Allowance (ESA)

Use this service if:

  • you need financial help because you are off work as a result of an illness, injury or disability, and
  • have paid National Insurance for 2 years

What to expect

This form is the first stage of your application. You will also need to go to an appointment to prove your identity and agree your responsibilities.

You might also need to go to an assessment.

Before you start:

Make sure you have your:

  • National Insurance number
  • bank or building society details
  • GP surgery name and address

You'll also need details of any:

  • current or recent work
  • pensions scheme's that you are currently paid from
  • permanent health insurance or income protection payments
