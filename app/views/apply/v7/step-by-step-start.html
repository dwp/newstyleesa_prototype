Apply for 'new style' Employment and Support Allowance

Use this service if you:

  • need money because you're off work with an illness, injury or disability
  • have paid National Insurance in the last 2 tax years

What to expect

This is the first stage of your application.

You'll also need an appointment to prove your identity and agree your responsibilities.

You might also need to go to a Work Capability Assessment.

Before you start

Make sure you have your:

  • National Insurance number
  • bank or building society details
  • GP surgery name and address

You'll also need details of any:

  • current or recent work
  • pensions that you're getting
  • permanent health insurance or income protection payments
