Before you start

Make sure you have your:

  • National Insurance number
  • bank or building society details (or those of a trusted family member or friend)
  • doctor's surgery name and address

Find out how to get a bank account if you do not have one.

You'll also need details of the following, if they apply to you:

  • current or recent work
  • the date any Statutory Sick Pay ended or will end
  • pensions that you're getting, including proof of any inherited pensions
  • permanent health insurance payments
