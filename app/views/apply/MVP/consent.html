Do you agree that your doctor or others named in this form can share information about your health condition that is relevant to your claim?

The Department for Work and Pensions (DWP), and any healthcare professionals approved by their Secretary of State, might need more information or evidence to support your claim.

The DWP will collect and store your consent on behalf of your doctor or any others you've named.
The DWP will make a decision on your claim using the information they have already.
What if I change my mind later?

You can change your mind at any time. If you do, the DWP will not ask your doctor or others to share any more information.

It does not affect the way in which they use your information.

You can do this at any time by calling 0800 169 0310 and saying you withdraw your agreement.
